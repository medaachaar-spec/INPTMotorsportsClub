About the Club

INPT Motorsports Club is a student community dedicated to motorsport, automotive culture, and racing technology. The club brings together students passionate about Formula 1, GT3 racing, GT4 racing, endurance racing, sim racing, karting, and car engineering.

Our goal is to create a space where students can share their passion for cars while discovering the technical aspects behind motorsport performance, such as aerodynamics, telemetry, race strategy, and vehicle design.
